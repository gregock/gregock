# Profile Positioning

- Approved title: Applied Systems Engineer
- Positioning: a hybrid operations-and-technology profile
- Not positioned as a traditional software engineer
- Builds practical operational systems, internal tools, decision-support systems, and AI-assisted workflow automation
- Background: law, business operations, customer-facing work, and direct operation of real workflows
- AI is used as a development partner for research, architecture exploration, implementation, debugging, testing, documentation, and iteration
- Gregorio remains responsible for requirements, workflow design, architecture, validation, operational judgment, privacy boundaries, and accepting or rejecting generated changes

## Target Roles

- applied systems
- technical operations
- implementation
- internal tools
- business systems
- AI-enabled workflow automation

## Employer Scope

Target employers are not limited to technology companies. Any organization with operational workflows that can benefit from systems and automation is relevant.

## Public Evidence Boundary

Public claims must remain proportional to available public evidence.
